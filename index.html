About Me

Hey, hey! Welcome to my website. My name is Tanisha and I'm an aspiring Full Stack Developer. When I'm not practicing coding, I am usually at work. I work with families in need to help them get back on their feet through removing barriers to their employment. Some families my have an education barrier or they may be in need of childcare assistance so they can look for and keep employment. I have been helping families toward self-sufficiency for 6 years now. This work very rewarding, but it can also be very stressful due to constantly "putting out fires" in people's lives while I'm in a deadend job. I'm seeking to learn something new and exciting; to increase my opportunities for growth in an ever-expanding field. Job secturity is very important to me. To be able to work from home or even while on vacation. (Wait...sctratch that because I'm not working while on vacation.)


Somethings I enjoy doing in my spare time is spending time with my daughter, Alana and painting. I love hanging out with my bright and bubbly 11 year old daughter and noticing the little, contant changes in growth that she developes. She has absolutely enjoyed drawing since she was a toddler and now easily draws all kinds of amazing original ideas and characters. My plan is to create a website for her to showcase her artwork in a portfolio.


If I had more time to paint, I certainly would. In my earlier years, drawing and creating charcoal drawings was my favorite passtime. My subjects are either flowers, like peonies or roses, as well as landscapes, animals and portraits.
